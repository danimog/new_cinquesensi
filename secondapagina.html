THE LOCATION

The place was an old cellar since the 18th century. Slate, wood, copper, antique tables and chairs; charming and exclusive. It is a little location hidden in a basement.We are not a restaurant or bar and we do not serve alcohol-free drinks, fruit juices or coffees.

WHAT WILL YOU TASTE AND BUY

Wines, spirits, cocktail and liqueurs, beer, local vegetable quiche, salted anchovies, charcuterie. Winetasting, olive oil tasting and pesto classes. Italian leather purses, local painting. And most likely, you’ll leave with a big smile on your face!

PRIVATE EVENT

Would you like to arrange a private wine tasting for you and your friends? For you and your spouse? A special wine tasting for your anniversary? When it comes to private tastings, anything is possible. We organize private wine tastings for groups everyday at every time.
